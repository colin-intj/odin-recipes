Dump and Bake Chicken Parmesan

Description

This is an easy chicken parmesan recipe; all one has to do is dump the ingredients into a dish and let them cook!

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 ounce) package whole wheat penne pasta
  • 1 (24 ounce) jar pasta sauce
  • 2–3 cups water
  • 1 pound of chicken (cut into small pieces)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella chees
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 218°C (425°F). Spray 9×13 baking dish with cooking spray.
  2. Add noodles, pasta sauce, water, chicken, and 1/2 cup of mozzarella cheese to the baking dish, then add salt and pepper. Mix the ingredients and make sure that liquid covers the chicken and pasta.
  3. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 20 minutes.
  4. After 20 minutes pass, uncover and bake for an additional 10 minutes—or until the chicken reaches 74°C (165°F).
  5. Spread breadcrumbs, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese over the pasta dish.
  6. Bake uncovered for 10 minutes—or until the cheese melts and the bread crumbs become golden brown.
