Easy Cheesy Lasagna

Description

A simple lasagna recipe which is much easier than most traditional ones.

The original recipe is by Kris Swiggum, which you can find at www.allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 pound of bulk Italian sausage
  • 1 cup of sliced black olives
  • 1/2 cup of warm water
  • 2 tablespoons of minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon of dried onion flakes
  • 2 teaspoons of garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons of ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of dried sage
  • 1 pinch of dry basil
  • 2 (14 ounce) jars of marinara sauce
  • 1 (16 ounce) package of lasagna noodles
  • 8 ounces of ricotta cheese
  • 1 pound of shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef and Italian sausage in a large skillet on medium-high heat until brown and crumbled
  2. Drain excess grease, then stir in olives and water
  3. Season the mixture with minced garlic, oregano, onion flakes, garlic powder, pepper, sage, and basil
  4. Simmer the mixture, stirring frequently for about 15 minutes; then stir in marinara sauce and remove from the heat
  5. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C)
  6. Spoon a layer of meat and sauce on the bottom of a 9×13 inch baking dish, then cover with a layer of noodles. Spread a thin layer of ricotta over it before sprinkling with some mozzarella
  7. Cover with another layer of marinara sauce, then repeat layering, ending with meat sauce on top. Reserve about 1/2 cup of mozzarella
  8. Bake in the pre-heated over for 45 minutes, checking after 30 minutes. You'll know that it's cooked when you can easily pierce it with a knife
  9. Sprinkle the reserved mozzarella cheese over the lasagna, then bake until the added cheese melts—this should take about 10 minutes
