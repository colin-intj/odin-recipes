Bacon-Wrapped Pineapple Burgers

Description

These are hamburgers with a uniquely Hawaiian twist. These burgers have pineapple on top, and they're wrapped in bacon.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup prepared barbecue sauce
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • Ground black pepper, to taste
  • 4 canned sliced pineapple rings
  • 8 slices bacon
  • Hamburger buns (optional)
  • Hamburger toppings (optional)
