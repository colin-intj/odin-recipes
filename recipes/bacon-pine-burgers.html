Bacon-Wrapped Pineapple Burgers

Description

These are hamburgers with a uniquely Hawaiian twist. These burgers have pineapple on top, and they're wrapped in bacon.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup prepared barbecue sauce
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • Ground black pepper, to taste
  • 4 canned sliced pineapple rings
  • 8 slices bacon
  • Hamburger buns (optional)
  • Hamburger toppings (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Set aside a large baking sheet with aluminum foil or parchment paper.
  3. Add the ground beef and barbecue sauce to a bowl, then mix them.
  4. Shape the beef mixture into four patties and season them with salt and pepper.
  5. Get eight strips of bacon and place each pair on the baking sheet so that they form X shapes.
  6. Place a patty on the center of each X, then place a pineapple ring on top of them.
  7. Wrap up each patty by folding up the ends of each bacon strip. Place toothpicks in the center of the patty to help secure the bacon.
  8. Bake the burgers until they're to your liking. To make the bacon crispier, place the patties under a broiler for 3–5 minutes after they're done cooking.
