Banana Bread

Description

This banana bread recipe is my all-time favorite! It's for the following Zojirushi breadmakers:

  • Home Bakery Supreme®
  • Home Bakery Mini
  • Home Bakery Virtuoso®
  • Home Bakery Supreme®

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1 cup ripe and mashed banana
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 3 tsp. baking powder
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Add all of the ingredients, except for the walnuts, to the baking pan.
  2. Place the baking pan into the Home Bakery and turn it on. Select the CAKE course, set the crust setting to LIGHT, then press the START button.
  3. When the breadmaker beeps, open the lid and use a rubber spatula to carefully scrape off any flour that might still be on the sides of the pan.
  4. Add the walnuts, then close the lid so the bread can continue baking.
  5. When it's done baking, allow the bread to cool before removing it from the pan.
  6. Cut the loaf into slices, then serve.

You can view the original recipe at www.zojirushi.com.
