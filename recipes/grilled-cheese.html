Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

A simple yet delicious recipe. It's so simple, in fact, that it's very description is in it's title!

The original recipe is by Sara, which you can find at www.allrecipes.com.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of white bread
  • 3 tablespoons of butter, divided
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all your ingredients
  2. Preheat a non-stick skillet over medium heat, apply butter to one side of a slice of bread
  3. Place slice of bread butter-side down in the hot skillet, and add one slice of cheese
  4. Apply butter to a second slice of bread, then place it on top of the cheese butter-side up
  5. Cook until lightly brown on one side, then flip over and continue cooking until the cheese melts
  6. Repeat with the remaining slices of bread, butter and cheese. Serve and enjoy!
