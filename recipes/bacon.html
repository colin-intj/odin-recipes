Bacon for the Family or a Crowd

Description

A delicious addition to any breakfast!

The original recipe is by JOYCE, which you can find at www.allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of thick sliced bacon

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C)
  2. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil
  3. Align the bacon strips in a single layer on the prepped baking sheet with the edges either touching or slightly overlapping
  4. Bake in the pre-heated oven for 10–15 minutes, or until it reaches the desired degree of doneness
  5. Remove the bacon from the baking sheet with tongs or a fork, and drain on a plate lined with a paper towel
